Trackerly

A Portfolio Tracker

For all of your investments.

Trackerly is an investment portfolio tracker app that integrates with many different platforms to allow a user to easily track all of their investments.
People often run into the problems making financial decisions with their entire portfolio in mind. With Trackerly, users are able to make informed decisions because they know all the information about their assets that are stored in multiple locations.

We use integrations from multiple platforms to track relevant portfolio data all in one place and provide the users with relevant stats and data to make informed financial decisions.

Features

Portfolio

Keep track of all of your assets in one place including bank account, exchange account, NFT, and crypto-wallets. All account balances can be imported by linking accounts with our app.

Watchlist

Build a watchlist. View and compare all stocks and cryptos you are focusing on at a glance with our real-time price services.

Newsfeed

Get today's business and financial news, plus the latest updates that may affect your money, investments, savings, and financial health.

Tax Calculator

Wonder the amount of taxes you are going to pay for your gain? No need to enter any of your information or gains. Our tax calculator does the job for you by retrieving the data from your account.

Notifications

Receive instant and personalized alerts about your account and the stocks you are interested in. No delays, instead, enhance your portfolio.
